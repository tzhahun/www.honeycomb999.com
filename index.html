New Opportunities for the

Production of Comb Honey

Our company creates and develops technology and equipment for the production of comb honey. Our latest design is a section made from the wood of birch trees, making it completely ecologically clean. We have begun the manufacture of serial sections and can offer them fully assembled. This allows the large scale production of honey in combs without unnecessary additional amounts of equipment or effort.


Using the technology for producing comb honey does not require equipment for pumping honey, and also significantly reduces the effort required to extract it. Honey in combs is guaranteed to be of high quality and have a long shelf life.

Technical description of a section:

Material – Birch
Weight - 20 (+ - 5) grams
Size - 114X67X37 (+-1) mm
Thickness – 2mm (+-0,3) mm
Holds honey - 220 (+ - 30) gram

Packaging

Our company can also supply final cardboard packaging for comb honey.
