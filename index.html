New Opportunities for the Production of Comb Honey

.

Our company devises and develops technology and equipment for the production of comb honey. Our latest design is a section (mini-frame) made from the wood of deciduous trees, making it completely ecologically clean. The aim of our company is to supply bee-keepers with everything they need to produce comb honey of the highest quality. We have begun the manufacture of serial sections and can offer them fully assembled. This allows the large scale production of honey in combs without unnecessary additional amounts of equipment or effort. Our company can also supply final card/cardboard packaging for comb honey.

Technical description:

Material – Birch
Weight - 20 (+ - 5) grams
Size - 114X67X37 (+-1) mm
Thickness – 2mm (+-0,3) mm
Holds honey - 220 (+ - 30) gram
