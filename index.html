NEW OPPORTUNITIES

FOR MAKING COMB HONEY

Comb honey is honey that is still in its beeswax honeycomb, just as the bees made it. It has not undergone any filtering or processing, it is honey in its most natural form.

Our company is all about comb honey; we design and produce equipment to make it easier for beekeepers, either hobbyist or commercial, to make and package traditional comb honey.

As you probably know, most honey nowadays is extracted from the honeycomb by centrifugal force before being put into containers. Making comb honey does not require a centrifugal honey extractor, but cutting comb honey from the frames into a convenient size for packaging can be a messy and wasteful business.

So we offer fully assembled sections within which the bees can build the honeycomb, which makes life a lot easier! It also enables the beekeeper to move the sections around in the super at any time.

Please watch this video to see them (and the bees) in action, as the honeycomb is built up inside them. Our sections are made out of wood from birch trees, so everything is kept ecologically clean.
